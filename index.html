My passion for Computer Science would make me reduce the fun I had because I desperately wanted to be in the program because my admission category was Life Sciences, not Computer Science (where I never had any prior background). Life was a little tough, adjusting to the new environment as well as working under pressure in a super competitive environment was a challenge. I had to zoom out, see what I had on my table, then tried my best to connect the dots.

Come mid 2022, I got admitted into Computer Science!! In my first year, I made 37 internship applications but received no interview for any of them. Following the first three months into my second year at the university, I made 77 applications, and was fortunate to receive one technical interview (with Google!!) which I passed. I accepted the offer and will be joining Google in the summer of 2023. I am looking forward for an exciting summer full of new experiences and endless learning opportunities!
